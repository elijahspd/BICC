There is no better way to discuss this topic without citing instances from a particular book, of course, the Bible - the Christians’ book, because the issue of leadership is thoroughly and deeply emphasized in the book, even from its beginning to its end. Even till now, God has hands in the ruling of man either directly or indirectly. God does not take the issue of leadership for granted; He appoints and relinquishes Kings and kingdoms because leadership counts in everything as everything revolves around the leader. He set up kingdoms and its rulers as well discard with them as He will and as determined by His set time and the purpose He intends to achieve.
