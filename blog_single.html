“Life is like a tale told by an idiot full of sound and fury” says William Shakespeare This greatest and highly revered literary enlightened mind got this simple logical analogy of life right; Story is account of real occurrence relayed while tales is past unreal events toldIn my own philosophy of life, you can only tell your own story but cannot tell your own tale.....

Many people are exactly as described by this our perhaps the greatest literary thinker in history “…a tale told by an idiot, full of sound and fury” This is more real in life than anyone can imagine Philosophically, sound may be a signal but in most cases it might make no sense Such that can make other sensible thinkers that hear it to become furious.

“ A church is a hospital for sinners, not a museum for saints. For where God built a church, there the Devil would also build a chapel.”

When death bell tolls on anyone, it will certainly brings about a circumstance The circumstance could makes no sense and could be ludicrous to human reasoning That makes people to make one comment or the other as if the victim was not smart enough Trying as much as possible to defend their perceived actions that could make them to avoid the same fate Making a god out of themselves as if they have absolute control over the affairs of their lives What they forgot to realize is that their own end may come through circumstance worse than the victim

They should also be made to understand that many people in history have fallen victims of death Through circumstances that were not directly linked to their personal faults or foolishness. But out of Society’s sheer negligence, government ineptitude/incompetence and insensitivity Insecurity, inefficient administration of Justice and personal interest above collective interest Corrupt practices and inordinate ambition of some sectional leaders in the country etc. Are all banes in our Country that could pull a string of death on anybody at any given point in time

People dying daily of accidents on our roads due to government insensitivity to our bad roads networks Petroleum tankers’ explosions, daylight armed robberies and kidnapping occur on our road frequently This is due to unorganized road transport system or administrations and insecurity Domestic gas explosion is a ghastly accident that when it occurs It will definitely claim the lives of the people involve, if at all lives were not claimed instantly.

When they were rushed to the hospital in pain, half-alive and half-dead by the good Samaritans The Doctor will demand that deposit should be paid before they can start the treatment This happens both in our public and private hospitals, even in other cases or scenario. What a crazy Society! in which premium is placed on money rather than human lives. A Society that idolize money will definitely not value human lives
